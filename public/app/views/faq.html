How can I make a difference?

Through our NGO you can donate to any of the projects of our partner NGOs and browse through the updates in that project. You can also volunteer for any of the projects according to requirement.

How can I stay connected to my seeded project?

Once you have become a part of any of the projects you will receive updates on that project and the progress made so far. You can also follow any of the projects which you have not seeded.

How am I guaranteed than my donation will actually help someone?

Our NGO is in partnership with some of the best and oldest NGOs in the country.These NGOs release all their data on their respective websites.

How can I register as a volunteer?

You can register yourself as a volunteer on any of the projects classified as in requirement of volunteers.

Can I directly contact the NGO about the projects?

Yes you can directly contact the respective NGOs about their projects but our website is designed in accordance to their strict guidelines.Using our website will make work easy for all these NGOs.

How many people can volunteer for a particular project?

All details of the projects are specified under the project description.

Can I leave a project for which I have volunteered in between?

All enrolments into projects should be done under good faith.You are morally and not legally under any form of contract.

To which NGO should I start donating?

All NGOs registered on are website are reputed NGOs all in need of great help.You can help any project you feel connected to.

Who can help?

To volunteer in any of the projects you should be at least 13 years of age and for donations at least 18 years.
